About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Stanford University, working under the supervision of Prof. Michael Chen. My research interests focus on machine learning, computer vision, and artificial intelligence applications in healthcare. I am particularly interested in developing robust deep learning models that can assist in medical diagnosis and treatment planning.

Prior to my PhD studies, I completed my Master's degree in Computer Science from UC Berkeley and worked as a software engineer at Google for two years, where I gained valuable experience in large-scale system design and machine learning infrastructure.
